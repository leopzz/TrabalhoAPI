Blush

Blush can be used when finishing make-up, it serves to give a blush to the skin ensuring a healthy skin color for the face. Another function of the face is to cooperate so that the makeup has a natural aesthetic. To use this product, a light brush stroke over the cheekbones makes all the difference.

Lipstick

Lipstick is a cosmetic used to color and enhance the mouth. It is presented in several colors, glossy or non-shiny versions, such as shimmering, opaque, moisturizing or glossy ones, in order to meet the most diverse tastes.

Bronzer

As the name implies, the bronzer aims to tan the skin. Generally, it has a lot of luminosity, that is, it brings a certain sparkle through glitter or other elements that cause the shiny effect.

Eyeshadow

Eyeshadow is a cosmetic product commonly used to make the eyes of the wearer stand out to appear more attractive, and should be applied to the eyelids and eyebrows. The eyeshadow adds depth and dimension, complements the color, or simply draws attention to the eye area.
